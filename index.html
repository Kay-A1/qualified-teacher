How It Works
Getting matched with your ideal tutor is easy — just three simple steps.
1

Get Started

Reach out via WhatsApp, phone, or our simple online form. Just tell us your child's needs and we'll take it from there.

2

We Handpick Your Teacher

Our team carefully reviews your requirements and selects the most suitable qualified teacher — not just by subject, but by teaching style and personality fit.

3

Book Your Free Trial

Your teacher will arrange your first session at a time that suits you. The trial includes a friendly introduction and a short diagnostic assessment. Your tutor will then provide personalised feedback and a tailored action plan, while giving you the opportunity to ensure they are the right fit for your child.

4

Choose Your Plan & Get Started

Select from a range of lesson packages to suit your child's goals and schedule. Once confirmed, your teacher will set up a consistent weekly timetable so learning stays on track.
