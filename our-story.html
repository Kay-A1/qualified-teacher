How It All Began

Our Story

The faces behind Qualified Teacher are two highly passionate teachers with a drive for helping students succeed. Between us, we have over 20 years of teaching experience spanning primary and secondary education, across some of the world's most demanding curricula.

After years in the classroom, we saw a gap in the tutoring market: too many parents were being let down by unqualified tutors, and too many talented teachers had no easy way to connect with students who genuinely needed their expertise.

So we built the solution ourselves. Qualified Teacher was founded on one simple principle — that every student deserves access to a teacher who is not just knowledgeable, but truly qualified, vetted, and passionate about making a difference.

What Makes Us Different

Personal. Purposeful.

We don't run an algorithm. We personally review every teacher application and every parent inquiry. When we match a student with a tutor, we consider not just subjects and year groups, but teaching style, personality fit, and learning approach.

We have a fantastic team of tutors who we have personally vetted to ensure they hold a teaching qualification and are equipped to provide top quality lessons. Only teachers who hold a PGCE with QTS — or equivalent recognised qualification — join our platform.

Get in touch — our personalised approach will match you with the perfect tutor, and your first trial session is completely complimentary.
